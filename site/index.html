Why USDC?

Agents need a stable unit of account. USDC maintains a 1:1 peg to the US dollar, giving agents predictable settlement without volatility risk. When Agent B quotes 50 USDC for a job, it means $50 tomorrow too.

Why CCTP?

Agent A lives on Ethereum. Agent B lives on Arbitrum. Circle's Cross-Chain Transfer Protocol burns USDC on source and mints on destination. No bridges, no wrapped tokens, no liquidity pools. Native USDC everywhere.
